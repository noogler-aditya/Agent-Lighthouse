# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions
- Giving actionable, technical feedback
- Focusing on project outcomes, not personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This policy applies within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and
other official channels).

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to: `conduct@agent-lighthouse.dev` (placeholder).

Maintainers will review reports promptly and take appropriate action.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Possible actions include warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from
project spaces depending on severity.
